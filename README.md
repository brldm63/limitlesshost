## What's the Catch?

Honestly? A few real things worth knowing:

**No free domain.** You'll need to register one separately. Domains start around $19/year for a .com — standard market rate, but it's not bundled.

**Support is semi-managed.** The team is responsive (ticket responses typically within a couple of hours), but they won't log into your site and fix things for you — they'll guide you, and you do the hands-on work. If you want fully managed support, that's an add-on.

**Shared hosting has resource limits.** Like every shared host on the planet. If your site suddenly goes viral and hammers the CPU, you'll hear about it. For small to medium sites — blogs, portfolios, small business pages — you'll almost certainly be fine. For high-traffic production apps, consider the VPS line.

**DirectAdmin instead of cPanel.** Limitless officially recommends DirectAdmin now, and has written publicly about why — cPanel's licensing costs have ballooned since 2019, and they're passing those savings on to customers. DirectAdmin handles everything you need. If you specifically need cPanel, they offer cPanel-based plans too, just at slightly higher pricing.
